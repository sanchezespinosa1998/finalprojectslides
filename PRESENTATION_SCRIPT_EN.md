# GreenThread — 5-Minute Script (Very Easy to Read)

25 slides, about 5 minutes.
Each line is short. Read one line, then breathe.
Switch the deck to EN (top-right button) before you present.

---

**Slide 1 — Cover (0:00–0:15)**
Good morning.
My project is GreenThread.
A sustainable fashion brand.
We sell online, direct to customers.

**Slide 2 — Agenda (0:15–0:25)**
My plan has four parts.
Study the market.
Build the strategy.
Take action.
Then measure it.

**Slide 3 — Context & Opportunity (0:25–1:00)**
Fast fashion is a big problem.
It creates a lot of CO2.
And a lot of waste.
People want to buy better now.
But it's hard to find the right brand.
Easy. Nice. Honest.
GreenThread is that brand.

**Slide 4 — SWOT (1:00–1:15)**
Our strength: a clear message.
Our weakness: a small budget.
Our opportunity: a growing market.
Our threat: big brands copying us.

**Slide 5 — Competitors (1:15–1:30)**
Zara is cheap.
But not very sustainable.
Reformation is sustainable.
But expensive.
We are in the middle.
Sustainable, and fair price.

**Slide 6 — Brand (1:30–1:40)**
Our mission is simple.
Sustainable fashion, made easy.
And made affordable.

**Slide 7 — Objectives (1:40–1:55)**
Our goals are clear.
Reach more people.
Grow our email list.
Get more sales.
Keep our customers happy.

**Slide 8 — Audience (1:55–2:10)**
We have three types of customer.
Laura wants style and values.
Carlos wants fast and easy.
Sofía wants a good gift.

**Slide 9 — Customer Journey (2:10–2:20)**
Every customer takes five steps.
Discover. Think. Decide.
Buy. Stay loyal.

**Slide 10 — TOFU: Awareness (2:20–2:35)**
First, we get noticed.
Instagram is our main channel.
TikTok helps us reach more people.
For free.

**Slide 11 — MOFU: Trust (2:35–2:45)**
Next, we build trust.
A welcome email.
Real reviews.
Small influencers.

**Slide 12 — BOFU: Sale (2:45–3:00)**
Then, we close the sale.
Easy checkout.
Free returns.
No stress.

**Slide 13 — Loyalty (3:00–3:10)**
Keeping a customer is cheap.
Finding a new one is not.
So we say thank you.
And we reward them.

**Slide 14 — Content (3:10–3:20)**
Most of our content helps people.
Only a little bit sells.

**Slide 15 — Creative Examples (3:20–3:30)**
Real videos.
Real questions.
Real answers.

**Slide 16 — Social Content (3:30–3:40)**
We post every week.
On Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest.

**Slide 17 — Email Automation (3:40–3:50)**
Four emails run automatically.
Welcome. Cart reminder.
Thank you. Come back.

**Slide 18 — Technical SEO (3:50–3:55)**
Google understands us better now.
That means more free traffic.

**Slide 19 — KPIs & Budget (3:55–4:10)**
Our budget: 780 euros a month.
By month three:
We expect 15,000 euros in sales.

**Slide 20 — Acquisition Model (4:10–4:20)**
More email subscribers...
...means lower cost per customer.

**Slide 21 — Business Model (4:20–4:35)**
Each customer is worth 110 euros.
We get our money back on sale number one.

**Slide 22 — Roadmap (4:35–4:45)**
Month one: set up and launch.
Month three: scale up.
Month twelve: strong brand.

**Slide 23 — Challenges (4:45–4:50)**
We know it won't be easy.
But we have a plan for that.

**Slide 24 — Conclusions (4:50–4:55)**
Three ideas hold this plan together.
Fair price. Full funnel. Real data.

**Slide 25 — Closing (4:55–5:00)**
That is GreenThread.
Thank you.
Any questions?

---

## Tips for reading it out loud
- One line = one breath.
- Speak slowly, even if it feels too slow.
- If you run short on time, skip slides 15 to 18.
- Smile before you say "thank you" at the end.
